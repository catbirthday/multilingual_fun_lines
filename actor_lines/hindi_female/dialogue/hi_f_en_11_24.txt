Character 2 [en]: Oh, but it's not though! See, traditional databases are like, mmm, filing cabinets, right? Everything has its place, super organized. Character 2 [en]: Right? And we're just scratching the surface. Wait until I tell you about what happens when you start connecting multiple lakes across different industries—that's when things get really, er, revolutionary.
