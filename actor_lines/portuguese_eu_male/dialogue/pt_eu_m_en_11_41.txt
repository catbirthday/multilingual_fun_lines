Character 1 [en]: So you admit the whole system is built on deception.

Character 1 [en]: That's a fantasy. Corporations don't do ethics.

Character 2 [en]: You're right that they're rare, and that's exactly why we need to support them! When companies see that ethical use of customer intelligence actually drives loyalty and profits, the whole industry starts shifting.

Character 1 [en]: The industry won't shift. There's too much money in exploitation.
