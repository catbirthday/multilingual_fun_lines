Character 2: Started with waltz because they said it was "beginner-friendly"—lies, by the way—but now I'm also attempting foxtrot and, uh, East Coast swing. The swing is actually more forgiving because you can kind of bounce your way through mistakes and call it "style."

Character 1: [joking] So you're only the second-worst now? That's progress!
