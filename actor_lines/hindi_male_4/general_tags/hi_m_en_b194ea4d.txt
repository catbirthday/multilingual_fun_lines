Character 2 [en]: [quietly] I didn't know that.

Character 2 [en]: [stunned] Why would you take this case on contingency when you know I've spent years trying to destroy your reputation?

Character 1 [en]: [thoughtfully] Because sometimes the best critics make the most important points, and frankly, someone needs to defend the people who keep us lawyers honest. [slight smile]

Character 2 [en]: [struggling] This is really hard for me to say, but thank you, and I'm sorry for calling you a soulless parasite in that June article.

Character 1 [en]: [wry laugh] Oh, that wasn't even your harshest review.

Character 2 [en]: [relieved exhale] So we're really doing this? You're going to represent someone who fundamentally opposes everything about how you practice law?
