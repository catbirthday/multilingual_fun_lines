Character 1: [firmly] This isn't one mishap—it's a pattern of deliberately circumventing privacy protections. [very sincerely] Look, I'll recommend leniency given your track record, but you need to understand the severity of what you've done here.

Character 1: [warmly] For what it's worth, you're a brilliant copywriter. [sighs] But in today's world, being brilliant means respecting the boundaries that protect our customers, even when those boundaries make our jobs harder.

Character 1: [surprised] Well, maybe that's where we start rebuilding. [pause] If you're willing to become our internal advocate for ethical data use, to help other teams understand these boundaries, that could factor into the board's decision.
