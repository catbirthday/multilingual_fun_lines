Character 1: Hey, I'm stuck on this game and I heard you're really good at walkthroughs?

Character 2: Yeah! You buy it from Sly after you rescue him. Makes finding collectibles way easier. Though honestly some people think using the map ruins the exploration aspect, which I kind of get.

Character 2: Classic mistake! The game doesn't really tell you about half the quality of life stuff. Like, did you know you can mark spots on your map with different colored pins? Total game-changer for backtracking.

Character 1: Where do I even find Sly?

Character 2: Got it. He's in the Forgotten Crossroads, in a little hut that you need to break into. You probably walked past it a dozen times without noticing.

Character 1: Is he hard to rescue?

Character 2: Oh absolutely. I've done complete guides for Axiom Verge, both Ori games, Bloodstained. Metroidvanias are basically my specialty since they all follow similar design patterns once you understand the formula.
