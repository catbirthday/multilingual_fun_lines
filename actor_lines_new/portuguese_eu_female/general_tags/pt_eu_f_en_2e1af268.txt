Character 1 [en]: [measured] I've been doing structural analysis for fifteen years, and hairline cracks don't heal themselves, especially not ones that were spreading at two millimeters per day.

Character 2 [en]: [deflecting] Well, the important thing is the bridge is safe now, right? No risk to the morning commuters, no catastrophic failure during rush hour.

Character 1 [en]: [grateful whisper] You saved all those lives, risked your license, your shop, probably federal charges if anyone found out what you did with those materials.

Character 1 [en]: [carefully] I'll file my report.

Character 1 [en]: [resigned acceptance] The scanner does have documented reliability issues, and the bridge is structurally sound now, which is what matters for public safety.

Character 2 [en]: [simply] Exactly right.
